Privacy Policy

RetireSmart · Last updated: June 2025

RetireSmart ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

RetireSmart is a local-first app. All financial data you enter (holdings, assets, liabilities, retirement goals, family profiles) is stored only on your device using your phone's local storage. We do not upload or transmit this data to any server.

  • Financial data — holdings, net worth, retirement goals, liabilities, other assets. Stored locally on-device only.
  • Profile data — your name and family member names. Stored locally on-device only.
  • Photos / screenshots — only accessed when you choose to import a holdings screenshot. Images are processed locally and never uploaded.
  • Stock price data — ticker symbols are sent to Yahoo Finance's public API to fetch live prices. No personal data is included in these requests.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share your data with any third party for advertising or analytics
  • We do not sell your data — ever

3. In-App Purchases

RetireSmart uses RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions and one-time purchases through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google/Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to purchase data: revenuecat.com/privacy.

4. Camera and Photo Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph or select a screenshot of your investment holdings for import. Images are processed on-device and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Yahoo Finance API — used to fetch real-time stock and ETF prices. Only ticker symbols are sent; no personal data.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Subject to their privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete on our end because we don't have one.

7. Children's Privacy

RetireSmart is intended for users aged 13 and older. The child profile feature within the app is for parents to model educational savings goals for their children — it does not collect any data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent update.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: momentsanctuary@gmail.com

It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, or any form of professional financial service.
