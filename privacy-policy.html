Privacy Policy

RetireSmart · Last updated: June 2026

RetireSmart ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you create an account. Used for authentication and account recovery only.
  • Display name — optional name you provide during sign-up. Stored in your account profile.
  • Financial data — holdings, net worth, retirement goals, liabilities, other assets, and family member profiles. Synced to our secure cloud database (Supabase) so your data is available across devices and restored after reinstalls.
  • Photos / screenshots — only accessed when you choose to use the AI holdings import feature. The image is sent to our server (Supabase Edge Function), which forwards it to Google Gemini for analysis. Images are processed in real time and are never stored on our servers or Google's servers beyond the duration of the request.
  • Stock price data — ticker symbols are sent to Yahoo Finance (for international stocks) or via our Supabase Edge Function proxy to Finnhub (for US stocks) to fetch live prices. No personal data is included in these requests.
  • Purchase data — subscription status is managed by RevenueCat and Google Play. We receive only a user identifier and entitlement status; we do not receive or store payment card details.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics
  • We do not sell your data — ever
  • We do not use your financial data for any purpose other than providing the app's features to you

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — your email is used to identify your account and allow you to sign in
  • Cloud sync — financial data, plans, and family profiles are synced to Supabase so you can access your data from any device and restore it after reinstalling
  • AI holdings import — screenshots you submit are sent to Google Gemini for ticker and share extraction, then discarded
  • Live prices — ticker symbols are sent to Yahoo Finance or Finnhub to retrieve current market prices

4. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data and account information are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and encrypted at rest. Supabase's security practices are described at supabase.com/security.

Some app preferences (such as dark/light mode and UI settings) are stored locally on your device and are not synced to the cloud. All financial data, notes, retirement plans, and goals are synced to Supabase and are available across devices.

5. In-App Purchases

RetireSmart uses RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions and one-time purchases through the Google Play Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to purchase data: revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Camera and Photo Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph or select a screenshot of your investment holdings for AI import. The image is transmitted securely to our server for processing and is not retained after the request is complete.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication. Your account and financial data are stored here. Privacy policy.
  • Google Gemini — AI vision model used to extract holdings from screenshots. Images are processed in real time and not stored. Google privacy policy.
  • Finnhub — US stock price data, accessed via our server-side proxy. Only ticker symbols are sent.
  • Yahoo Finance — international stock price data. Only ticker symbols are sent; no personal data.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Subject to their privacy policy.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the app via Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account from our authentication system and deletes all financial data, plans, and family profiles from our database. The deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Locally stored preferences (dark mode, net worth history) are removed when you uninstall the app.

9. Children's Privacy

RetireSmart is intended for users aged 13 and older. The child profile feature within the app is for parents to model educational savings goals for their children — it does not collect any data directly from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent update.

11. Contact Us
